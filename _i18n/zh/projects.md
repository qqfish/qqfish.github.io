
### Haijiao EDU (WebFC)

An online education [website](www.haijiaoedu.com) to provide one to one tutoring service. There are two major components: an online communication tool and service website.

1. Online communication tool

    It allows multiple users to video chat in the same room. They can also share, view and modify pdf file simultaneously with others. It will automatically load all bookmarks in the pdf file, which help user searching needed content. In additon, user can download current version of file anytime he wanted.

2. Service website

    It provides services for two kinds of users, student and teacher, to help them find the proper tutor or student. And It also contains an online payment service to manage all their cash.

    For a teacher, just after a few simple steps, he can register himself on our website, which will allow students to buy his courses. He can also search the student he can teach and contact the student.

    For a student, he can search all teachers on our website and also register some tutor requirmentA
